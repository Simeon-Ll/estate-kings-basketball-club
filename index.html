About Estate kings

Estate Kings, a local grassroots basketball club, was founded in [year] by a group of passionate basketball enthusiasts in Abesan estate locality. With just a handful of eager kids and a single outdoor court, they aimed to provide a safe and nurturing environment for young athletes to develop their skills and foster a love for the game.

Over the years, Estate Kings grew steadily, attracting more children and dedicated coaches who volunteered their time. In 2021, African Giants made a mark in their lives providing them with modernized utilities , marking a significant milestone in its history. This allowed them to offer year-round training and expand their programs, including youth leagues and skill development camps.

Today, Estate Kings stand as a thriving grassroots basketball club, boasting a diverse and dedicated group of young athletes. It continues to be a source of pride for our community, nurturing both basketball talent and life skills, with a bright future ahead.
